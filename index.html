Introduction

Afghan proverbs carry the wisdom, humor, and culture of generations. They teach patience, resilience, and kindness through simple, memorable sayings.

Proverb Gallery

صبر تلخ است اما شیرین می‌شود

Patience is bitter but it becomes sweet.

This proverb teaches that patience is difficult at first, but rewards are worth the wait.

آب که یک جا بماند، می‌گندد

Water that stays in one place becomes stagnant.

It means that staying idle leads to problems; movement and change are vital.

زبان سرخ سر سبز می‌دهد بر باد

A sharp tongue can destroy a green head.

Be careful with harsh words, as they can ruin relationships and opportunities.

با یک دست نمی‌توان دهان را پوشاند

You cannot cover your mouth with one hand.

Secrets are hard to keep; truth will eventually come out.

گر صبر کنی ز غوره حلوا سازی

If you are patient, you can make sweet from sour grapes.

Patience can turn bad situations into good ones.

دوستی بهتر از دارایی است

Friendship is better than wealth.

True friends are more valuable than riches.

کار نیکو کردن از پر کردن است

Good work comes from doing repeatedly.

Practice makes perfect.

هرکه بامش بیش برفش بیشتر

Whoever has a higher roof has more snow.

Greater responsibilities bring more challenges.

About This Project

Afghan Proverbs is a cultural project created by Noorani Asghary to preserve and share the timeless wisdom of Afghanistan. These proverbs reflect generations of experience, humor, and deep thought in just a few words.
